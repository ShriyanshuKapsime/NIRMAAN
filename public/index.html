Three Steps to Transparency

A simple workflow connecting every stakeholder in the development process.

Step 1

Admin Assigns Work

The administrator creates projects and assigns construction tasks to verified contractors.

Step 2

Contractor Uploads Progress

Contractors document and upload real-time progress with photos, reports, and milestones.

Step 3

Citizen Verifies Reality

Citizens verify on-ground progress, report discrepancies, and hold stakeholders accountable.
